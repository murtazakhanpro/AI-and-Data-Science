[2] THE 9-STEP WORKFLOW MAP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 1. Pick ONE Niche      -> Master a single high-value skill.
 2. 5-Day Upwork Study  -> Search 12-1pm daily. Log jobs, demands, and budgets.
 3. Google Trends Check -> Filter 'Worldwide' + 'Past 30 Days' to find hot zones.
 4. Build Landing Page  -> Use Carrd/Canva. Add Headline, Portfolio, and CTA.
 5. Lead Generation     -> Paid (Facebook/Google Ads) OR Free (Upwork, LinkedIn).
 6. First-Touch Message -> Ask a simple question. Do NOT pitch your services yet.
 7. Discovery Call      -> Listen 80% of the time. Find out what hurts their business.
 8. 4-Part Proposal     -> Send: Problem -> Timeline -> Your Solution -> Price.
 9. Get Paid via USD    -> Route client funds through Payoneer to Pakistani Bank.

 * Graphic Design    : Branding, logos, and social media layout designs.

[4] THE OUTREACH SECRET (ASK, DON'T PITCH)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 * BAD  : "Hi, I have 5 years of experience. Please check my portfolio and hire me..."
 * GOOD : "Hi [Name], I saw your page and liked [Specific Thing]. Quick question: 
           Are you happy with how your site is converting viewers into customers?"
 * WHY  : It is about them, references a specific point, and asks an easy question.

[5] FINANCIAL PIPELINE (GETTING THE DOLLARS HOME)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 * Infrastructure : PayPal does not work in Pakistan. Use Payoneer.
 * Account Setup  : Sign up free with CNIC. Get virtual USD/GBP/EUR/AED numbers.
 * Bank Links     : Connect Payoneer to Upwork, Fiverr, or give directly to clients.
 * Withdrawal     : Pull money to HBL, Meezan, or UBL. Converts at live PKR rates.
 * Pro-Tip        : File your taxes for free and save your remittance certificates.
